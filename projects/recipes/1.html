Key Lime Pie Recipe

Return home

Description... This recipe is intended for a 9 inch pie.

Ingredients

Name Amount Purpose
Graham Cracker Crumbs 1¾ cups Crust
Brown Sugar ⅓ cup, packed
Melted Unsalted butter ⅓ cup
Key Lime Juice ½ cup Filling
Sweetened Condensed Milk 14 oz
Egg Yolks 4

Servings: 8–12
Prep time: 45 minutes

Instructions

Part 1: The crust

  1. Preheat oven to 375 ºF.
  2. Combine the graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and butter all together, and then mix.
  3. In a 9" pie pan, use your new mixture to create the sides and base of your pie crust. The layer should be ¼" thick.
  4. Bake for around 10 minutes, then let it cool.

Part 2: The filling

  1. Combine the lime juice, condensed milk, and yolks, then mix.
  2. Pour the mixture into the piecrust.
  3. Bake for 10–15 minutes (still at 375 ºF), and then let it cool.
  4. Refrigerate for at least 3 hours.

Tips & Substitutions

  • If you're feeling lazy, you could always go out and buy a premade graham cracker piecrust instead of making your own.
  • While key limes are the titular choice, any type of lime juice should do, really.
  • For extra flair, use some lime zest in your filling and/or as garnish.
