Clam Chowder Recipe

Return home

Description

Ingredients

Name Amount Purpose
Bottled Clam Juice 1½ cups Base
Half-and-Half 2 cups
Heavy Cream 1 cup
Unsalted Melted Butter 3 tablespoons
Minced Clam 25 oz Solids
Potatoes, Peeled and Cubed 4 cups
Bacon Strips, Diced 4
Onion, Chopped 1½ cups
Salt 1½ teaspoons Seasoning
Pepper ¾ teaspoon

Servings: 8
Prep time: 40 minutes

Instructions

  1. Put bacon and onion pieces into a large stockpot, and cook for around 5 mintues.
  2. Add salt and pepper.
  3. Add in potatoes and clam juice, then let simmer for roughly 15 minutes.
  4. Pour in the half-and-half, heavy cream, and melted butter.
  5. Add clam, continue cooking without boil for 5 minutes.

Tips & Substitutions

  • Spice things up by adding corn kernels and/or chopped bell pepper.
  • Bacon may be substituted for ham.
  • Popular potato choices are baby red and Yukon gold, though some favor russet potatoes for their starchiness.
  • If you're feeling fancy, put the chowder in a sourdough bread bowl!
