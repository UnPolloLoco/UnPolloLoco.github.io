Network Notes

Vocabulary

  • Protocol: A rule or requirement.
  • Packet: A small piece of information, often paired with other packets to communicate a larger message.
  • Header: The metadata of a packet.
  • Payload: The actual data in a packet.
  • Router: A thing that conveys packets.
  • Routing Table: A thing that decides where packets should go.
